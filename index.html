About InterCraft

Looking for a Minecraft Server? InterCraft is for you. If you're looking for a chill server to relax on, we got you. If you want somewhere to grind with friends, we've got you too.

Who We Are

InterCraft is a community for playing games, making friends, and having fun! Whether you want to practice PvP, build a town, or just chat with people from around the world, InterCraft is for you.

The goal of InterCraft is to provide a place where anyone can do anything, anytime.
